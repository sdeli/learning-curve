    FYI, the added weight of the gloves in the back of your hands keeps the weights in place when you throw your punches. Since punching moves are already trained regularly with your normal speed, you will have no problem with the additional weights.

    Some good reasons to include the weighted gloves for your boxing drill:

    What Why

    Increase your stamina Because you have been training with resistance and therefore once the resistance is removed you can go longer.
    Increase strength and power Because you are performing high numbers of repetitions and increasing the size of the muscle cells.

    monday

    On Monday, I usually work out quite lightly and only starting to wear the weighted gloves on the last part of my exercise, so the format is:

    • • 10 minutes stretching and warming ups
    • • 10 minutes shadow boxing
    • • 10 minutes heavy bag punching
    • • • 10 minutes coached mitt works
    • • 5 – 10 minutes slow boxing with partner using the first 1 lbs. weight

    Tuesday:

    On Tuesday, I do the same format like the first day with only increasing the power on the last part of weighted boxing, rather than doing a slow boxing drill I do more normal sparring with my partner, (and maintain that 10 minutes sparring, for the next month training wearing heavier weights).

    Wednesday:

    This third day, I emphasize more on the quality of my punching accuracy, so I do a coached mitt boxing with my experienced coach, Kris, for 15 minutes and continue with 15 minutes of punching power building by hitting the heavy bag, and then I end it up with the 15 minutes of boxing with my sparring partner, so it is a five rounds fights. All of this drill is wearing the weighted gloves.

    Thursday:

    This is my favorite day, after having enough starters (warming ups) I do a 7 rounds (the threshold of my stamina) fight wearing the weights. I can feel the pressure of this 7 round a lot more, due to the additional weights on my hands. And that is concluding my 4th day.

    Friday:

    This last day of my weekly exercise is the time to test my 4 days training, so I concentrating this Friday to do a 12 round boxing without the weighted gloves, and of course after having enough stretching and warming up. I can tell you that you will find the difference having a fight without the weights, It is so much lighter and powerful. I can finish the 12 round much better than before.

    I spend the weekends traditionally for watching boxing video, have a lunch with friend or family, and having fun but definitely no alcohol, no fat burger – because I have to maintain my stamina and my performance, if I do it than I will lost the whole of my boxing training hard work.

    Then on the second week I do the same training formats as the first week, I only increase the weights on the third week, using the 2 lbs. weight and then continue implementing this training program for the whole week. And this happens until the end of the first month.

    Everytime I finish wearing the weights and fight with normal gloves I can feel the big changes of my boxing quality, I can hit the heavy bag harder and swing it at least 20% further on the first month and about 40% on the second month, and even better in the third month with approximately 60% -70% compared to the month before I use the weighted gloves.

    With this training I also bettered my lethal weapon, a finishing right hand straight punch. I like the combination of jab, double uppercut, and hook to open my opponent's double cover and finish it with my hard straight punch.

    So after the three months drill with finally putting on the 3lbs weights my killing straight punch was really improved, my boxing quality, my footwork was so agile and I can do a 12 round moving and punching, and throwing 30 – 50 powerful punches in every round, that is almost 10 – 20 more punches in one round than before the training, and I didn’t feel really weak after a full 12 rounds. Training with the weights gloves have helped my footwork trained to move much more, and when all weights are taken off I can move much faster and lighter.
