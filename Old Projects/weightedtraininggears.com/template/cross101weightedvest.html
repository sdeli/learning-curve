The Cross101 Weighted Vest

Are you an athlete who has unorthodox training methods? A weight room, or traditional gym just isn’t going to get it done for you; you’re someone who needs to be out and about or in the ring or arena training. If this sounds like you, you’re looking for a weighted vest to add resistance to fighting, CrossFit, or wrestling moves!

The Cross101 weighted vest is the perfect investment as far as weighted vests go because it has a myriad of awesome features. There are ten 4lbs weights that can be added and subtracted to allow a resistance from 0lbs to 40lbs. The entire weighted vest is a fully adjustable vest with a middle strap that secures it snugly in place, no matter your size. This weighted vest is truly a one-size-fits-all product.

This ensures that no matter how you wear it, or what conditions you wear it under, it will be form fitting and snug so that it wont bounce around, wont weigh down your shoulders or neck, because it is evenly distributed across your body. The Cross101 weighted vest is made of such a high quality durable material that it is fully backed by a manufacture warranty to replace the vest if it rips, tears or cracks. If you’re looking for resistance to your: combat sport training, hiking, CrossFit, or wrestling training, than look no further. The Cross101 weighted vest is all that you will ever need.
