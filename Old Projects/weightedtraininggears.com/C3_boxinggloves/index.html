    producers description - EVERLAST C3 PRO - WEIGHTED BOXING GLOVES

    With Everlast C3 Pro weighted Boxing training gloves, it will be possible to boost your speed along with power constantly. These training gloves come with six inserted weights that are completely removable. The inserted weights are distributed evenly in the entire glove and this provides added resistance to all the boxing training workouts. The weights can be added gradually in the gloves as one becomes more congenial with heavier gloves. Everlast C3 Pro training gloves are fully made up of leather. And its leather construction depicts that these gloves will last for long. One can wear these gloves anytime while training hard and heavy.


    Everlast C3 Pro weighted Boxing training gloves come with C3 foam technology. Such a technology offers enhanced cushioning, support as well as force distribution. The C3 foam is to provide extreme protection and increase power in each punch. The centralized balanced placement of weight is featured to offer maximum dispersion of force. However, the removable inserted weights are of 6.5 oz each, four in the wrist and two in the palm. These training gloves weigh 16oz (1lb) and 56oz (3.5lb) without weights.

    The gloves feature full hook and loop wrist Velcro wrap closure. This provides excellent support to the wrist during heavy impacts. However, these gloves come only in one design and size. This means, that one size will fit all hands.
